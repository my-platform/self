Robot For Serving and Securing Elderly

Overview

This robot is produced to provide two functions

1- Service function:
Basically its made for serving the elderly and the sick and disabled, but we're decided to develop it to be flexible to be used also in malls and large stores as a guide for customers can also be used in museums as a tour guide instructs tourists from places monuments inside museums, temples and can through screen installed on the body Android display information about all traces of the relics inside the museum as well to explain an audio recorder.

2- Security function:
It's equipped with a complex artificial intelligence system to work as a home gard, instead of making it a minor domestic use to protect the elderly and the sick and disabled, we decided to expand slot Consumer recipient of our products, by marketing the robot for companies and factories and shops and banks and policing institutions and help the police and armed forces.
